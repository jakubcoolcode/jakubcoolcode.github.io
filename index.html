About us

Experience, quality and expertise with a professional approach to business and cool approach to life - this is us - Cool Code. We're passionate about what we do and have bag of experience both in European and local projects. Now we want to bring it to you. We hope you're excited as we are!
In our ranks we have software engineers, graphic and UX designers, social media specialists and a range of partners we could bring on board if needed. We can help you with small stuff like website designs, through WordPress installations, administration of your e-commerce business and mobile applications, to complex software systems tailored to your needs. You say what you need and we deliver it. Isn't that cool?
